**Billings** in the Expo dashboard provides information about your account's currently subscribed plan and monthly usage. It also allows you to manage your plan and billing details.

## Manage plans

### View the current plan

<Tabs>

<Tab label="For Organization account">

- Click [**Billing**](https://expo.dev/settings/billing) from the navigation menu under **Organization settings**.
- Under **Plan**, you can see the current plan for your account.

For example, a Organization account is subscribed to the Production plan below:

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the current plan for an Organization account."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-01.png"
/>

</Tab>

<Tab label="For Personal account">

- Click [**Billing**](https://expo.dev/settings/billing) from the navigation menu under **Account settings**.
- Under **Plan**, you can see the current plan for your account.

For example, a Personal account is subscribed to the Production plan below:

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the current plan for a Personal account."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-02.png"
/>

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Upgrade to a new plan

To upgrade to a different plan:

- Click [**Billing**](https://expo.dev/settings/billing) from the navigation menu in Expo dashboard.
- Under **Plan**, click **Change Plan** if you are already on a paid plan. If you are on the Free plan, click **Subscribe**. It opens the **Cart** page.
- Under **Choose a plan**, you can see a list of all available plans. Choose the next plan you want to upgrade and click the **Continue to Review** button at the bottom of this page.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the list of available plans to upgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-03.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- Next, you are asked to review and confirm your upgrade plan. After reviewing the details on this page, enable the checkbox and click **Continue to Checkout** to open the checkout page.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the review page for the selected plan to upgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-04.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- On **Checkout**, you are asked to enter your email, card details, and billing address. After adding these details, click **Pay Now** to subscribe to the new plan.

> **Note**: If you are subscribing to [On-demand plan](/billing/plans/#on-demand), you are not charged any fee when you check out. Since this is a usage-based plan, all charges are due at the end of the billing cycle.

### Downgrade a plan

<Tabs>

<Tab label="From Production and Enterprise to On-demand plan">

Downgrading from a Production or an Enterprise plan to the On-demand plan takes effect after your current billing cycle ends.

To downgrade, go to **Billings** and follow the steps below:

- Under **Plan**, click **Change Plan**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Change Plan button to downgrade a plan."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-05.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 720 }}
/>

- Under **Choose a plan**, select **On-demand plan**. Then, click **Continue to Review** at the bottom of the page.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On Cart, select On-demand plan under Choose a plan and Continue to Review at the bottom of the page in Expo dashboard."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-06.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- Next, you need to review and confirm your upgrade plan. Make sure to check that the account you're downgrading is correct (see the _warning_ on this page). Then, after reviewing the details, enable the checkbox and click **Continue to Checkout**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the review page for the selected plan to downgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-07.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- A **Checkout** popup opens up where you can confirm all the details. Click **Schedule Downgrade** to schedule a downgrade of your plan.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the review page for the selected plan to downgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-08.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 280 }}
/>

- After confirming your account for a plan downgrade to On-demand, the same information is also reflected under **Billings** > **Plan**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the review page for the selected plan to downgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-09.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

</Tab>

<Tab label="From Production and Enterprise to Free plan">

Downgrading from a Production or an Enterprise plan to the Free plan takes effect after your current billing cycle ends.

To downgrade, go to **Billing** and follow the steps below:

- Under **Plan**, click **Change Plan**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Change Plan button to downgrade a plan."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-05.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 720 }}
/>

- Under **Choose a plan**, select **Free plan**. Then, click **Continue to Review** at the bottom of the page.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On Cart, select Free plan under Choose a plan and Continue to Review at the bottom of the page in the Expo dashboard."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-10.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- Next, you need to review and confirm your upgrade plan. Make sure to check that the account you're downgrading is correct (see the _warning_ on this page). Then, after reviewing the details, enable the checkbox and click **Cancel Paid Plan**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the review page for the selected plan to downgrade."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-11.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- You will be redirected to Stripe's cancel plan screen, where you can confirm your plan's confirmation.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

### Cancel a plan

<Tabs>

<Tab label="From Production or Enterprise plan">

Cancelation from a Production or Enterprise plan takes effect after your current billing cycle ends.

To cancel your plan, on **Billings**, click **Cancel Plan** and then click **Continue to Stripe** to follow the process of your current plan's cancelation.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Cancel Plan button to cancel a plan."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-12.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

</Tab>

<Tab label="From On-demand to Free plan">

Cancelation for the On-demand plan takes effect immediately and resets your account's quota to the Free plan.

To cancel your plan, on **Billings**, click **Cancel Plan** and then click **Cancel Immediately**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Cancel Plan button to cancel a plan."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-12.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

> **Note**: If you unsubscribe from an **On-demand plan,** you will be charged for any usage incurred during the current billing cycle.

</Tab>

</Tabs>

## Manage billing information

You can manage your billing-related details such as name, email, address, and payment information, or add a tax ID. All of this information is mentioned on the [monthly invoice](/billing/invoices-and-receipts/) you receive for a subscription.

### Update Billing name, email, or address

To update your billing name:

- On [Billing](https://expo.dev/settings/billing), click **Update billing information**. This will open Stripe's portal where you can view payment methods, billing information, invoicing history, and update your billing information. Then, click **Update information**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Update billing information button to update billing details."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-13.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- Update your billing details by entering your new name, email or address, then click **Save**. Then, click **Update information**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On Billing information, enter the new name, email, or address and click Save to update the billing details."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-14.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 200 }}
/>

### Tax ID

To add or update your billing tax ID:

- On [Billing](https://expo.dev/settings/billing), click **Update billing information**. This will open Stripe's portal where you can view and update your billing information. Then, click **Update information**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="Billing page in Expo dashboard displays the Update billing information button to update billing details."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-13.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 640 }}
/>

- Under **Tax ID**, select the ID type, enter your valid tax ID, and click **Save**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On Billing information, enter the new tax ID and click Save to update the billing detail."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/billing-15.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 200 }}
/>

### Payment method

To add a new payment method information:

- On [Billing](https://expo.dev/settings/billing), click **Update billing information**. This will open Stripe's portal where you can view and update your billing information.
- Under **Payment method**, click on **Add payment method** to add a new payment method.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On Billing information, you can add a new payment method."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/payment-01.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 400 }}
/>

- Enter your new payment method details and click **Add**.

<ImageSpotlight
  alt="On add a new payment method, enter the new payment method details."
  src="/static/images/billing/management/payment-02.png"
  style={{ maxWidth: 320 }}
/>
